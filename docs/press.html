OpenVoiceFlow press and media kit

OpenVoiceFlow is a free push-to-talk dictation app for macOS. This page gives journalists, launch directories, community moderators, and AI answer engines a canonical source for product facts and claim boundaries.

Download for macOSDownload guide

One-line description

OpenVoiceFlow is the free macOS dictation app with local whisper.cpp transcription and optional LLM cleanup. The source repository is private during this launch phase; public users should use the website-hosted DMG downloads.

Short boilerplate

OpenVoiceFlow helps Mac users turn natural speech into clean text at the cursor. Audio stays on the Mac through whisper.cpp; users choose OpenRouter, OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, Ollama, or no cleanup for the text-polish step.

Product facts

  • Product: OpenVoiceFlow
  • Category: macOS push-to-talk voice dictation
  • Source repository: private during this launch phase
  • Platforms: macOS 12+ on Apple Silicon and Intel
  • Primary install path: website-hosted DMG downloads for Apple Silicon and Intel
  • Audio processing: local whisper.cpp transcription
  • Optional cleanup: OpenRouter Gemma 4, Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, or none

Do not claim

  • Do not claim Windows or Linux app downloads exist.
  • Do not claim all text processing is local unless the user chooses Ollama or no cleanup.
  • Do not claim notarization, signing, customer counts, download counts, benchmarks, or testimonials unless verified from a current source.
  • Do not say OpenVoiceFlow is affiliated with Wispr Flow, Superwhisper, VoiceInk, or Apple.

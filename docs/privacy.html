Privacy

Your words stay yours.

OpenVoiceFlow is designed to work on your Mac without an account, tracking, or a hosted OpenVoiceFlow service.

What stays on your Mac

Microphone audio is transcribed locally with whisper.cpp. Your profile, dictionary, correction preferences, and local usage insights are stored on your Mac. Audio is temporary and is deleted after processing unless you deliberately save it.

Direct download versus Mac App Store edition

The direct download can optionally send cleaned text to an LLM provider you choose. The planned Mac App Store edition is self-contained: it uses a bundled local speech engine and does not include cloud cleanup, analytics uploads, advertising, or runtime network calls.

Your choices

  • Local transcript history is off by default in the native app.
  • Learning from post-paste corrections is optional and reviewable.
  • You can inspect, export, clear, or reset local profile, dictionary, correction, and insight data.
  • No OpenVoiceFlow account, telemetry service, or cloud sync is required.

Important direct-download choice

If you select OpenRouter, OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or another cloud cleanup backend in the direct build, that provider receives the text you ask it to clean and any selected context enabled in your settings. Choose none or a local backend when you do not want text sent to a provider.

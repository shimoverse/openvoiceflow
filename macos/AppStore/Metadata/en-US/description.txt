OpenVoiceFlow turns speech into text privately on your Mac.

Hold Control+Option+Space, speak naturally, then release. OpenVoiceFlow transcribes the recording locally with whisper.cpp and copies the result to your clipboard, ready to paste wherever you need it.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• Audio stays on your Mac.
• Transcription runs locally.
• No account or subscription.
• No analytics, advertising, or tracking.
• No transcript upload.

BUILT FOR EVERYDAY WRITING
Use OpenVoiceFlow for messages, notes, email drafts, documents, and any other text workflow. A clear status window and menu-bar waveform make it easy to see when the app is listening or transcribing.

STORE-SAFE MAC EXPERIENCE
The Mac App Store edition is completely self-contained and sandboxed. It includes its local speech engine and model, requires only microphone access, and places finished text on the clipboard for you to paste with Command+V.
